Block Blast

Tetris is all fun and games, but the pressure of dropping blocks quickly? Oh, it's not what we love in a chill puzzle game, right? So, we've created Block Blast Online, offering endless Tetris fun without any time limits. Take it easy, and place the blocks in the grids in peace. No mechanics will rush you. Just fill a row or a column in the grid to clear the blocks. It's puzzle-solving at your own pace!

Controls
Use your mouse to drag blocks
