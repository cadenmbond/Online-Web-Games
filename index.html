About Silly Games

Silly Games is a free to play games website, containing lots of different games without the hastle of navigating through ads.

Silly Games is a single person project created for a summative assignment in
Mrs. Gledhill's ICS3C1-1-Introduction to Computer Programming class at
Maple Ridge Secondary School.

Does my progress save?

Since games are embedded and rendered on behalf of www.onlinegames.io, their site handles all of the saving and retrieving data, this site is merely a collection of games put together from their website.

Do I need an Account to Play?

An account is NOT required to play any of our games, owning an account is also not required to save your progress. You can continue to play games regardless of whether you have an account or not. HOWEVER your data will NOT save across devices.
